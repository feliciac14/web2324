A Reflection on My First Year of Knitting

the "boat neck top" by nelsknits

What I've Made This Year:

A v-neck sweater vest

This was NOT it. It fit very baggy and not in a good way... it was a trash bag silhouette. It was my first experience knitting ever, so it holds a special place in my heart. For some reason my tension was so good? I was onto something that I'm no longer on. I love the yarn though, so I'm planning on scrapping it to make the Boat Neck Top by nelsknits. This might take a while because I'm currently taking a break from knitting, the folklore cardigan drained me.

Leg warmers

Why??? Why did I make these? I used this black and grey yarn from the Makerspace and knitted two small tubes. Firstly, I rarely wear skirts/dresses. Secondly, the legwarmers didn't keep my legs warm! They are now lying forgotten in the back of a cabinet. Thin, poorly seamed tubes of knit. I can't express my disgust and hatred for them enough.

Folklore cardigan

THIS WAS IT! I spent the majority of 2023 working on this cardigan and it is so beautiful! The cables, the moss stitch, the ribbing with black stripes! Chef's kiss! I am not exactly a Swiftie, but the design of her cardigan is pretty cool. TO THE TAYLOR FANS READING THIS, there is a video on her that you should watch. It's highly interesting and enlightening! Neocapitalism! Poptism vs. Rockism! The Beatles! This is my favorite knit of 2023. My favorite knit ever!
