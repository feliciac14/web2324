YEAR 1!

This website is a collection of all my knitting knowledge and a documentation of my first year of knitting.

I've learned a lot this year

    A few things that I've learned are:
  • How to read patterns
  • Different stitches
  • How to make cables, with and without a cable needle!
  • The power of blocking!
  • Continental vs. English knitting

    • Pictured to the left is a part of my folklore cardigan. I'm making it based off of the "Folklore Cardigan" knitting pattern by Lion brand. This cardigan has taught me a lot. I learned how to adjust a pattern to my measurements from it, which was a little scary, but blocking is a savior! I learned how to knit cables with a cable needle at first, but then I learned how to knit cables without a cable needle. I learned about moss stitch, garter stitch, all of that good stuff!
